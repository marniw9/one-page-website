Mile High Run Club

Mile High Run Club is the first of it's kind: a studio dedicated solely to running. Boasting 36 top-of-the-line Woodway treadmills, 10+ classes daily and two prime locations, this runner's paradise is ideal for when the weather's rough or your just want the extra motivation of a group environment. MHRC offers three different classes for runners of all levels: Dash 28, High 45 and The Distance. Dash 28 begins with 28 minutes of structured running -- think intervals, hills and speed work -- followed by 10 minutes of runner-specific strength and kettlebell exercises. If you're after a little more mileage, High 45 provides runners with 45 minutes of uninterrupted treadmill time. The Distance is the for the truly ambitious athlete, covering 60 minutes of running composed of longer, more challenging intervals.

What to Bring: Running clothes, sneakers, water bottle (lockers are electronic)

Website: http://www.milehighrunclub.com/

NOMAD Location: 24 W 25th St, New York, NY 10010

NOHO Location: 28 E 4th St, New York, NY 10003
